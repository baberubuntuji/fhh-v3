Heated Hoses

We design a vast range of heated hoses that are suitable for maintaining a temperature during transport of adhesives, oils, chocolate, gas, emulsions and alimentary, cosmetic and pharmaceutical products.

Features

All our heated hoses are constructed using the highest quality materials

They are designed to be flexible and strong

They are able to withstand high temperatures and pressures

Our heated hoses are suitable for any liquid or gas transfer , from household water transfer to bitumen in road constructions, to gas transfer in gas analysers, to hot melt in plastic industry

All our heated hoses are sheathed with heating elements and contain temperature probes to ensure the fluid viscosity is maintained over long distances

Basic hose construction

We use a PTFE lined hose (1) with Stainless Steel braid (2) to give hose pressure.

Hose is wrapped with protective Glass Tape (3) and a sensor (4) if fitted (taped) on to the core. The heater wire (5) is evenly coiled around the hose and is then wrapped in a further layer of glass tape, this being held with high temperature give.

The wrapped hose is covered in a high temperature insulation (6) made of closed cell silicone tube, which is waterproof. Next, a protective braid (7) is applied to the outer hose as protection.

Finally, high temperature end caps (8) are fitted to complete the hose.

Standard specification

The standard specification for our heated hoses is shown below. However, we can manufacture "any length, any size" and should you have any specific requirements outside the ranges specified below, please feel free to contact us!

  • Operating Voltage
    Typically 115V to 440V
  • Maximum Operating Temperature
    600 °C
  • Temperature Probe
    Thermocouples, thermisters, PT100, Ni120, etc.
  • Tube Diameter
    Typically 1mm to 55mm
  • Outer Jacket
    Nylon woven protective braid in black or grey
  • Inner Tube
    PTFE tube smooth bore or convoluted, with upto 6 layers of Stainless Steel overbraiding. We also manufacture Stainless Steel convoluted cores
  • Heating Element
    Spiral wound resistive heating element
  • Thermal Insulation
    Silicone sponge closed cell for upto 220 °C, and Nomex for higher temperatures up to 600 °C
  • Maximum Length
    Typically from 6 inches to 60 - 70 feet
  • End Fittings
    Virtually any industry standard end fitting can be supplied
